About Us

Government Polytechnic, Karad (GPK)is one of the oldest technical institutes in the Satara district of Maharashtra, established in 1957. The institute was the academic brainchild of late Y.B. Chavan, the first Chief Minister of Maharashtra, who envisioned social reformation through technical education for the state's rural population Originally starting with only a Civil Engineering diploma in Ogalewadi, the institute later moved to its current campus in the educational hub of Vidyanagar, Karad. Today, it is a premier public institution approved by the AICTE and affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE)..
